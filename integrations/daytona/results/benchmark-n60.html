Do the fixes actually move the outcome?

Each task is a known failure with a known fix, run in isolated cloud sandboxes across a control arm and a treatment arm. The verdict is flywheel's own paired-bootstrap scorer — and it will only say helped when the effect clears a measured A/A noise floor at n≥60. Gold, by construction.
